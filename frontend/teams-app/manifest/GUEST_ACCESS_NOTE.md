# Guest Access Note

- Outside users must be invited as Guest users in the Kent Business College tenant.
- They must accept the guest invitation before testing the Teams app.
- They must switch organization in Microsoft Teams to `Kent Business College`.
- They should be added to at least one Team in the tenant before testing team or channel surfaces.
- The meeting organizer must add the app to the meeting before the guest joins.
- External or anonymous joins can have a reduced Teams app experience even when the QR page itself is working.
